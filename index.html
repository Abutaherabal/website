Always up-to-date dependencies, zero hassle.

Tired of keeping all your dependencies up to date? Uncomfortable with just pinning them? Yup, so are we. So we made greenkeeper: it knows when your dependencies get updated and just sends you a pull request with the new package.json. If you've got continuous integration tests, they'll run, and you can keep your software up to date with a single click on the merge button.

Flippin' sweet, how does it work?

Easy: Open your terminal, and

$ npm install -g greenkeeper

Then inside the repo you want to keep up to date, do

$ greenkeeper enable
